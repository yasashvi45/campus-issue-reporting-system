Admin FAQs

How do I assign an issue to a specific staff member?

Go to "Manage Issues", click "Update" on the issue card, and select the staff member from the "Assign Staff" dropdown.

Can I export all issues to an Excel file?

Yes, you can use the "Export CSV" button on the Manage Issues page or the "Export Report" quick action on the Dashboard.

How do I disable a student account?

Navigate to the "Students" page and click the "Disable" icon (user-forbid) next to the student's name.
