Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to resolve an issue?

Resolution time depends on the priority and category of the issue. Emergency issues are addressed immediately, while low-priority issues may take up to 48 hours.

Can I edit or delete my reported issue?

Yes, you can edit or delete your issue from the "My Issues" page as long as its status is "Pending". Once an admin starts working on it, you can no longer modify it.

How will I know when my issue is resolved?

You will receive a notification in the app when the status of your issue changes. You can also track the progress on the "My Issues" page.
